# Privacy Policy — Jira Estimate Summary

_Last updated: 2026-06-10_

**Jira Estimate Summary** ("the extension") respects your privacy. This policy
explains what data the extension handles and how.

## Data collection

The extension does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal or user data
to external servers. There is no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party
services.

All data from your Jira boards (estimates, logged time, assignees) is read and
processed **locally in your browser** to display per-column summaries. This data
never leaves your device and is never sent anywhere.

## Local storage

The only data stored by the extension is your local configuration (display
settings), saved in Chrome's local storage on your own device. It is used solely
to remember your preferences between sessions and is never transmitted.

## Permissions

- `storage` — used only to save your local settings on your device.

The extension runs on Atlassian Jira pages (`*.atlassian.net`) and local
development hosts to read board data already visible to you on the page.

## Data sharing

We do not sell, transfer, or share any user data with third parties. We do not
use user data for any purpose unrelated to the extension's single purpose, nor
for creditworthiness or lending.

Any changes to this policy will be published on this page.
